The Campus Academic Resource Sharing Platform is a web-based application developed to support and enhance the academic experience of college students by providing a centralized digital space for sharing and accessing learning materials.

The platform allows students to upload, browse, download, and manage various academic resources such as class notes, previous year question papers, study materials, reference documents, project reports, and assignment solutions.

Privacy and access control are key features of the platform. Students can mark resources as private or public, ensuring secure and fair sharing of academic materials.

üéØ Purpose of the Website

  • To provide a centralized digital platform for academic materials
  • To reduce dependency on physical notes and photocopies
  • To encourage peer-to-peer knowledge sharing
  • To help students prepare better for exams and projects
  • To save time by making resources easily searchable and downloadable
